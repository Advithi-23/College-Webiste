Teaching and Learning Tools at KIT

BH ROAD, TIPTUR, TUMKURU(TUMKUR), KARNATAKA, 572201

To leverage a diverse array of teaching and learning tools to enhance the educational experience for students. Central to this approach are Learning Management Systems (LMS) like Moodle, Canvas, and Blackboard, which serve as digital hubs for course materials, assignments, discussions, and grading.
These platforms facilitate seamless communication between instructors and students, offering a centralized space for accessing resources and tracking progress. Complementing LMS are video conferencing tools such as Zoom and Microsoft Teams, which have become indispensable for hosting live lectures, virtual office hours, and collaborative group discussions, especially in the context of online and hybrid learning models. These platforms foster real-time interaction and engagement, transcending physical boundaries to connect students and faculty from diverse locations.
