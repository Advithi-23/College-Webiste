About Kalpataru Institute of Technology

The undergraduate programs of Computer science & Engineering and Electronics & communication Engineering received an accreditation by National Board of Accreditation (NBA) for three years (2022-23, 2023-24 and 2024-25)..

KIT's Vision

Our Vision is to bring forth Technical Graduate of high caliber with a strong character and to uphold the spiritual and cultural values of our country.

KIT's Mission

Our mission is to impart quality technical and managerial education at Graduate and Post Graduate levels through our dedicated and well qualified faculty members.
